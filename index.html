Our Solution

To address the mass incarceration problem and high rate of recidivism in our criminal justice system, we offer a comprehensive solution that we call Through the Gateway.

  • High-Tech Courses

    We offer university-level courses in high-tech skills such as Data Science, Web Development, UX/UI Design, Database Management, etc. Courses are taught by instructors with proven track record of professional and pedogical experience.

  • Real-world Projects

    Collaborating with local technology companies, we design our courses with real-world problems . These projects enable our students to build up their professional portfolio of projects to showcase their talent to prospective employers in the tech industry.

  • Re-entry Transition

    We help our students prepare for career and life outside of correctional facilities by coaching them in interview skills, resume writing, business ethics, etc. through one-to-one mentoring and group workshops.

  • Internship

    Through collaboration with our member companies, we offer full-time, paid internships to our students upon their release. This enables them

  • Career & Life Mentorship

    We continuously engage our students even after their release from correctional facilities and follow up on their careers. We provide additional workshops such as personal finance, entrepreneurship, etc.

  • Building A Community

    We strive to build a community of alumni who finished our Throught the Gateway path and are working professionals in the technology industry. We hold monthly coding nights where the alumni can come together and code together in a collaborative setting. We encourage collaboration and entrepreneurships through the alumni network.
